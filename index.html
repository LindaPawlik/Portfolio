Hello, my name is Linda Pawlik; a graphic designer from St. Paul, Minnesota. A lifelong artist, I owned and operated a retail floral shop for 25+ years. I love art in all of its many forms, including digital. I started painting and drawing when I was still quite young, always loving the freedom art allowed me in expression. I am also a digital artist, and am Adobe Certified in Photoshop, InDesign, Illustrator, and Professional Visual Design.
